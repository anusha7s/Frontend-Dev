Understanding Web Design Principles

By Anusha Saraswat | Published on October 29, 2025

Design is not just about looks; it’s about how things work. A good design simplifies user interaction and provides a seamless experience.

Consistency in layout and typography helps users navigate and absorb information effectively.

“Design is intelligence made visible.” – Alina Wheeler

Accessibility ensures that everyone, regardless of ability, can use and benefit from your website. This is essential in inclusive web development.

Modern web design embraces responsiveness, ensuring layouts adapt to all devices, enhancing usability and engagement.
